Why?

Demystify is an effort to understand how and why things are made and how they affect the geographical, political and social fabric of society.

A camping festival like no other

Set amid the hills near Bangalore, it's a perfect escape from the urban routine. Set and setting.

Making. Demystified.

Learn how your body works through movement. Brew your own beer. Bake bread. Make your own furniture. The sky is the limit. Make something new.

Discuss the politics behind making

Things aren't made in isolation. Whatever we make affects people in a myriad ways; the social and power dynamics between people; our personal privacy, agency and freedom. There's much at stake.

A way of life

Learn how to grow your own food, compost your waste, repair electronics, recycle and upcycle. We're going to have to find more sustainable ways of living. Fast.

Principles

  • Respect personal space

    Demystify is meant to be a safe and inclusive space. Always ask before engaging with someone's personal space. Respect their decision. Demystify will have awareness and child-care teams on site.

  • Care for the environment

    Do not bring any plastic that will be disposed on site. Leave the place better than it was.

  • Engage

    Demystify is a highly participative event. Propose a session, make something, engage in discussions and help put an amazing event together. Passive participation is highly discouraged.

  • Make it your own

    Demystify is what we make of it. We live together for the weekend. Look out for each other, treat it like home.
