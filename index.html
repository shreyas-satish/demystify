What is Demystify?

demystify is an effort to understand how and why things are made, and exploring more sustainable alternatives to popular things on the market.

Being able to understand how things work and making our own versions is empowering. It lets us shape things so that they work in ways we want them to. And repair them when they break down.

Exploring why things are made is important too! Things aren't made in a vacuum. Hence, understanding historical context and anticipating unintended consequences goes a long way.

demystify is taking shape as a camping festival near Bangalore and is a highly participative event. The agenda is largely informed by what the participants want to explore.

Making from scratch. Demystified.

Making things from scratch can be a whole lot more fun than just, you know, buying them. Especially when we collaborate with other fun people!

Take a workshop on weaving hats and lamp shades from the local crafts center, learn how to work with wood and bamboo or understand how you can setup a network in the country side. Make something new.

revive repair culture

Just how making things ourselves doesn't have to be a mystery, repairing them doesn't have to be either. Junking things instead of repairing them has an environmental cost too.

Learn about how you can repair your own electronics and carry out basic home repairs.
