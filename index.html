Making. Demystified.

Urban lifestyles can tend us towards taking things as is. Ever wonder about how things are grown, made and distributed?

Pick something you're interested in learning more about, and make it with fellow campers. Learn how to grow vegetables, bake bread or brew your own beer. Make your own furniture. The sky is the limit. Make something new.

Explore the politics around making

We don't make in isolation. Whatever we make affects people in a myriad ways; the social and power dynamics between people; our personal privacy, agency and freedom. For instance, in what ways could a national identification project affect a billion lives?

Repair. Recycle. Reuse.

Learn how to repair electronics, recycle and upcycle 'waste'. The environmental changes on our planet mean we're going to have to find more sustainable ways of living. Fast.

A camping festival like no other

Set amid the hills near Bangalore, it's the perfect escape from the urban routine. Making, art, nature and friendly company. Set and setting.

Guidelines at camp

  • Respect personal space

    Demystify is meant to be a safe and inclusive space. Always ask before engaging with someone's personal space. Respect their decision. Demystify will have awareness and child-care teams on site.

  • Care for the environment

    Do not bring any plastic that will be disposed on site. Leave the place better than it was.

  • Engage

    Demystify is a highly participative event. Make something, present a session, engage in discussions or volunteer and help put an amazing event together. Passive participation is highly discouraged.

  • Make it our own

    Demystify is what we make of it. We live together for a weekend, look out for each other and treat camp like home.
